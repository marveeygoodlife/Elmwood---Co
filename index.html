Our Story

Elmwood & Co. started with a simple belief — furniture should be built, not manufactured. Every chair we make begins as raw timber, shaped through joinery techniques passed down rather than assembled from pre-cut parts. We work in small batches, by hand, because furniture meant to outlast trends shouldn't be rushed through a production line.
